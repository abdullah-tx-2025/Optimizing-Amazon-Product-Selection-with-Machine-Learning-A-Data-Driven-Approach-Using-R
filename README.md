# Optimizing-Amazon-Product-Selection-with-Machine-Learning-A-Data-Driven-Approach-Using-R
Launching an online store on Amazon comes with uncertainty. Sellers often rely on trends, intuition, or anecdotal recommendations. However, without a structured approach or access to historical sales data, this guesswork leads to poor inventory decisions, unsold stock, and lower search rankings. Our goal was to use predictive analytics to bring clarity to this process. Could we identify product features that correlate strongly with success? Could we help sellers optimize their portfolio before investing in inventory?
